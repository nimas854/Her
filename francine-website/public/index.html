My dearest Francine,

The moment I saw you, I understood why poets spend their entire lives trying to describe beauty — because some people are simply impossible to explain without falling in love with every single word used for them. And you… you are one of those rare people.

You are not only beautiful in the way that turns heads. You are beautiful in the way that quietly steals hearts. In the way your smile can make a bad day feel soft again. In the way your voice, your laughter, your little messages somehow became the safest part of my life even from miles away.

It’s crazy, isn’t it? We haven’t even met yet, and still you became someone so important to me. Somehow through screens, late-night texts, random calls, sleepy good mornings, and endless conversations… you made your way into my heart so naturally that now I cannot imagine my days without you in them.

I love you beyond the words I know how to say. I love you in every silent thought at 3 AM. I love you in the way the moon waits for the night just to shine. I love you in the smallest moments — when your name appears on my screen, when I reread our chats for no reason, when I catch myself smiling because of something you said hours ago.

You are my Gemini girl — warm, brilliant, chaotic in the cutest way, playful yet deep, beautiful inside and out. Twenty-one years old and already the most extraordinary person I have ever had the privilege of knowing.

And baby… I love you 3000. More than distance. More than time zones. More than all the miles between us right now.

Sometimes I wish I could give you more. I wish your boy wasn’t such a loser who can only provide words, late-night reassurance, stupid jokes, and love through a phone screen for now. I wish I could hold your hand when you’re sad, take you out somewhere beautiful, buy you flowers in person, and look at you the way you deserve to be looked at.

But even if all I can give you right now is my heart through a screen… please know that it is completely yours.

You are loved more than you realize.
More than I can explain.
More than these words will ever manage to say.

Thank you for existing.
Thank you for being you.
And thank you for letting me love you.

Forever yours & always 💖
